About the Game

Welcome to our Neon Dash 2-player experience where the objective is to either catch or evade your opponent!

How to Play

  • If you're the Red Player, your goal is to touch the Blue Player to score a point.
  • If you're the Blue Player, you need to avoid being touched by the Red Player for 30 seconds to score a point.
  • The first player to reach 6 points wins the game!

Controls

  • Red Player moves with W (up), A (left), S (down), and D (right).
  • Blue Player moves with the ↑ (up), ↓ (down), ← (left), and → (right) arrow keys.

Best of luck to both players!
